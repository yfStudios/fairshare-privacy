Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

FairShare ("FairShare", "we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that helps couples and households track shared expenses and calculate income-based contributions to a joint account. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data.

By using FairShare, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

1. Who we are

FairShare is operated from France. The data controller for the personal data processed through the app can be reached at fairshare.hq@gmail.com.

2. Age requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to use FairShare. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.

3. Data we collect

We only collect what we need to run the app. Specifically:

Data you provide

  • Account information — your email address (required) and a display name (optional), used to identify you within your household.
  • Authentication data — if you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive a unique user identifier and your email from that provider. We do not receive your password.
  • Household financial data — expenses, incomes, budget targets, transfer records, contribution plans, and any notes you choose to add. This data is entered by you and shared with the other members of your household.
  • Household configuration — household name, currency, timezone, and the list of members you invite.

Data collected automatically

  • Push notification token — if you enable push notifications, we store a device token issued by Apple or Google so we can deliver in-app alerts. We do not use this token for any other purpose.
  • Authentication metadata — Firebase Authentication records the timestamp of your last sign-in and basic security signals to detect abuse.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your precise or approximate location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, calendar, or files.
  • We do not run third-party advertising or marketing analytics.
  • We do not collect crash logs, behavioral analytics, or device fingerprints beyond what Firebase needs for authentication and security.
  • We do not collect bank account, credit card, or any payment data — FairShare does not process payments.

4. How we use your data

We process your data only to:

  • Provide the core features of the app: expense tracking, monthly summaries, and contribution calculations.
  • Synchronize data between members of the same household — both partners see the same records.
  • Authenticate you and protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Send push notifications you have explicitly enabled (e.g. "your partner added an expense").
  • Respond to support requests and account deletion requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for marketing.

5. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, our legal bases for processing your personal data are:

  • Performance of a contract — to deliver the Services you signed up for (account, household sync, calculations).
  • Consent — for push notifications, which you can revoke at any time in your device settings.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Services secure, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • Legal obligation — when we must retain or disclose data to comply with the law.

6. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the European Union (multi-region eur3: Belgium and the Netherlands). Firebase encrypts data at rest and in transit. Access to your household's data is enforced by server-side security rules — only authenticated members of your household can read or write it.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication, which may process limited data (email, user ID, sign-in timestamps) on Google's global infrastructure. Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, providing an adequate level of data protection for any transfer outside the EU.

7. Who we share data with

We share data only with the third-party processors needed to operate the app. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

  • Google Firebase (authentication, Firestore database, cloud functions) — processes account and household data on our behalf.
  • Expo (push notifications) — delivers push messages if you enable them. Receives only the push token and the message payload.
  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Apple Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Apple (iOS only).

We may also disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of FairShare, our users, or the public.

8. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (in the app: Settings → Delete account), we permanently delete your personal data and your household's records within 30 days, except for limited information we are required to keep for legal or security reasons (e.g. to prevent abuse).

You can also email fairshare.hq@gmail.com to request deletion if you cannot access the in-app option.

9. Your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area, the UK, or another jurisdiction with similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In France, this is the CNIL.

To exercise any of these rights, email fairshare.hq@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security

We apply industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, server-side access rules that scope data to your household, and regular review of our authentication and authorization controls. No system is perfectly secure — if you become aware of a vulnerability or a suspected breach involving your account, please contact us immediately.

11. Shared household data — important

FairShare is a shared household app. Any expense, income, budget, or note you record is visible to all members of your household. If you leave a household, the records you created during your membership remain in that household's history. Only members of a household can read or modify its data — but within a household, there is no privacy between members. Choose your household members carefully.

12. Cookies and tracking

FairShare is a mobile app and does not use browser cookies. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. We do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals because we do not track you across services.

13. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use of FairShare after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

14. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or to exercise any of the rights described above, email us at fairshare.hq@gmail.com.

You can also review our Terms of Use.
