Delete your FairShare account

App: FairShare · Developer: yfStudios · Last updated: 2026-05-03

This page explains how to request deletion of your FairShare account and the data linked to it. You can do this from inside the app at any time, or by emailing us if you can't access the app.

Delete your account from inside the app

  1. Open the FairShare app and sign in.
  2. Tap the More tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Tap Account, then Delete my account.
  4. Read the on-screen confirmation, then type DELETE to confirm.
  5. Tap Delete my account. The deletion runs immediately.

If you can't access the app

Email fairshare.hq+support@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign up. Include the subject line "Delete my FairShare account". We will manually delete your account within 30 days of receiving your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your user profile (display name, email).
  • Your authentication account (Firebase Auth) and all active session tokens.
  • If you are the only member of a household: every record in that household — expenses, transfers, contribution plans, budgets, categories, savings pots, and the household record itself. This is a permanent hard delete.

What is kept (and why)

  • Tombstoned membership in shared households. If you share a household with other members, your identity link (name + email) is removed, but your past financial activity inside that household (the expenses you logged, contributions you made, transfers you recorded) is retained so that the remaining members keep an accurate financial history. The tombstone shows as "Former member" with no personally identifiable information.
  • Anonymized server logs required for security and abuse prevention (App Check attestation failures, rate-limit hits). These contain no personal data and are retained per Firebase defaults.
Note for shared households. If you want every trace of your financial activity removed from a shared household — not just your identity — you must first leave the household (which keeps shared history intact) or ask the other members to delete the household entirely. Email us if you need help arranging this.

Retention period

In-app deletion runs immediately. Email-requested deletion is completed within 30 days of the email arriving. Tombstoned records (see above) are retained for as long as the shared household exists, with no personal identifiers attached.
